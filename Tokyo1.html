Tokyo Workshop #1

We are excited to announce that we will be hosting our next workshop in Tokyo on May 27th! (12:00-14:00pm.) The workshop will be hosted inside StudioIrodori. The address of the studio is as follows: 東京都港区麻布十番１丁目５−１ 麻布十番YKビル3F Google Maps Link: https://goo.gl/maps/3dD3vf5rFzKauPAD8

Google Maps View

To get to the studio, it takes about 4 minutes by walk from Exit 7 or 5a of Azabu-juban Station. Walk along Tokyo Route 319 toward Toriizakashita (intersection) and Roppongi Tsutaya. The first floor is Roppongi Ashi Yuyuu and there is a sign "Ashi Yuu" on the first floor. (You will be facing Azabu-juban station, just before the Roppongi Tsutaya bookstore.)
