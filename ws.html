Our Workshop Schedule

Nagano Workshop #1

Nov 21st, 2022

In November, we hosted our first ever workshop in our Nagano Branch! This workshop featured our first implementation of contact improvisation.

Nagano Workshop #2

Jan 23rd, 2023

In January 2023, we hosted our second Nagano workshop. Thank you to all of those people who attended both of our Nagano workshops!

Nagano Workshop #4

May 26th, 2023

On May 26th, we hosted our 4th workshop in Nagano! In this workshop, we taught a new choreography with a new song, and received wonderful feedback from our participants. We would like to thank the participants once again and we look forward to hosting more workshops soon!

Tokyo Workshop #1
