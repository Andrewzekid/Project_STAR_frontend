Building Connections
through Dance

Project STAR is all about raising awareness of speech impediments such as stuttering and how to empower those that experience it. Therefore, we aim to help those with speech impairments through contact improvisation as a way for them to gain confidence. Our workshops range from 90-120 minutes long, and we have recently held two workshops in Nagano. Moreover, we are working on hosting a new workshop in Tokyo on May 27th, where anyone is welcome to join!

Nagano Workshop #1
Nov 21, 2022

On November 21st, we had the honor to host our first workshop at our Nagano branch. The workshop was a 1 hour and 30 minutes comprehensive lesson on stuttering and contact improvisation. We really loved the supportive atmosphere and would like to thank everyone for participating!

Hosting Workshops Around the world

Project STAR is beyond excited to announce that we now have new branches in Beijing and the United States! So far, Project STAR has hosted 6 workshops in Nagano, 1 workshop in Tokyo, and 1 workshop in Beijing. We now have branches in Tokyo, Nagano, Beijing and the United States.
